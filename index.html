Zero Tolerance for Child Sexual Abuse & Exploitation (CSAE)

📋

About This Document

This app does not host user-generated content or social/dating features. Regardless, is fully committed to child safety across all its applications.

🚫

Prohibition of CSAE — Child Sexual Abuse & Exploitation

  • Any content, behavior, or activity that sexually exploits or abuses minors is strictly forbidden.
  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) is banned in any form, without exception.
  • Any user found attempting to use the app to facilitate CSAE will be reported to authorities immediately.
  • Complies with all mandatory child safety reporting obligations.
  • Violations result in immediate account termination and law enforcement reporting.
🛡️

In-App Reporting Mechanism

This app provides users with a clear and accessible mechanism to report child safety concerns:

  • An in-app Report / Feedback option is available in the app's settings menu.
  • All child safety reports receive the highest priority and are reviewed promptly.
  • Reports involving CSAM or CSAE are immediately escalated to NCMEC and relevant law enforcement.
  • Users can also report directly via the contact email below.

Legal Compliance & Declarations

The developer declares that this app:

  • Complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations in every region where the app is available.
  • Maintains a process for detecting, removing, and reporting CSAM if ever discovered or reported.
  • Reports CSAM and CSAE incidents to NCMEC and regional authorities as required.
  • Will cooperate fully with Google, law enforcement, and relevant agencies in any child safety investigation.
  • Regularly reviews and updates these standards to remain compliant with Google Play policies.
